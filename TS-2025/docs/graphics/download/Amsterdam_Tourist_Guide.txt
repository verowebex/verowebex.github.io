### **Tourist Attractions**

1. **Rijksmuseum**
   - **Address**: Museumstraat 1, 1071 XX Amsterdam
   - **Directions from RAI**:
     - **Public Transport**: Tram 4 to Vijzelgracht, then a 5-minute walk (20 minutes).
     - **Taxi/Uber**: Approx. 10 minutes.

2. **Van Gogh Museum**
   - **Address**: Museumplein 6, 1071 DJ Amsterdam
   - **Directions from RAI**:
     - **Public Transport**: Tram 4 to Vijzelgracht, then a 7-minute walk (20 minutes).
     - **Taxi/Uber**: Approx. 10 minutes.

3. **Anne Frank House**
   - **Address**: Westermarkt 20, 1016 GV Amsterdam
   - **Directions from RAI**:
     - **Public Transport**: Tram 4 to Dam, then a 10-minute walk (25 minutes).

4. **Bloemenmarkt (Flower Market)**

7. **Artis Royal Zoo**
